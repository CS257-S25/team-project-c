Welcome to the UFO Sightings App

Use the following URL patterns to view sightings data:

  • /sightings/year/<year>
    Example: /sightings/year/1999
    Year must be between 1941 and 2013.
  • /sightings/shape/<shape>
    Example: /sightings/shape/circle
    Shape must be one of the following:
    • circle
    • disk
    • light
    • fireball
    • triangle
    • oval
    • cylinder
    • rectangle
  • /sightings/topdata - View the top 5 years and shapes by number of sightings.
